Mahindra Singh Dhoni

cricketer

MS Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains. He led India to victories in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. Known for his calmness and finishing skills, he also captains Chennai Super Kings in the IPL. Dhoni retired from international cricket on August 15, 2020.
